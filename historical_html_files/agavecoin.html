About AgaveCoin

AgaveCoin price today is $0.061916 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $21,794.53 USD. AgaveCoin is up 5.05% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #293, with a market cap of $24,185,645 USD. It has a circulating supply of 390,619,593 AGVC coins and the max. supply is not available. The top exchanges for trading in AgaveCoin are currently Sistemkoin, Uniswap (V2), Dcoin, Coinlim, and Folgory. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

The AgaveCoin token (AGVC) is an ERC20 token created to support the agave industry Agavecoin Global S.A describes itself as a Mexican industrial company with an international presence that aims to become a worldwide producer of Agave-based Products (e.g. tequila , mezca). AgaveCoin aims to present a solution for the transaction of agricultural products and services in the agave industry.
