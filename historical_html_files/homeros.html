About Homeros

Homeros price today is $0.837696 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $6,228,727 USD. Homeros is up 1.44% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #208, with a market cap of $312,451,813 USD. It has a circulating supply of 372,989,310 HMR coins and a max. supply of 10,000,000,000 HMR coins. The top exchanges for trading in Homeros are currently VinDAX, VCC Exchange, DigiFinex, Coinsbit, and LBank. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

Homeros offers a platform intended for both game developers and gamers. Homeros equips game developers with the freedom to develop and launch their games with the assurance that all data and information are stored, utilized, and protected within the blockchain. As a decentralized community, Homeros provides a space for investors and developers to collaborate in making games through initial game offerings (IGO).
