About ZB Token

ZB Token price today is $0.241587 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $8,477,380 USD. ZB Token is down 0.02% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #77, with a market cap of $111,924,533 USD. It has a circulating supply of 463,288,810 ZB coins and the max. supply is not available. The top exchanges for trading in ZB Token are currently XT, ZB.COM, ZBG, Bitfinex, and Hotbit. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

ZB Token (ZB) is an exchange token and the native currency of the ZB.COM crypto exchange. The ZB can be used to purchase monthly VIP status plans for transaction fee discounts, vote on exchange decisions, gain early access to special ZB launchpad events, receive crypto rewards from seasonal buybacks, and trade with other cryptocurrencies listed on the ZB exchange.
