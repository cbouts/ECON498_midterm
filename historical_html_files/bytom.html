About Bytom

Bytom price today is $0.053016 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $16,373,314 USD. Bytom is down 1.12% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #102, with a market cap of $73,404,177 USD. It has a circulating supply of 1,384,573,769 BTM coins and a max. supply of 2,100,000,000 BTM coins. The top exchanges for trading in Bytom are currently Huobi Global, OKEx, HBTC, Xtheta Global, and CITEX. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

Bytom is a blockchain-based open-source value-exchange protocol. It aims to tokenize real world assets, such as warrants, securities, dividends and bonds. Users can use the Bytom blockchain to create assets without limits and have these assets circulate freely.

Bytom has developed a series of products in addition to the Bytom blockchain. A DeFi protocol - MOV, open cross-chain gateway OFMF (Open federation Management Framework), enterprise blockchain - Bystack, a BBFT + DPoS High speed sidechain - Vapor, Data service provider Blockmeta, Mobile Wallet - Bycoin, Chrome extension wallet - Byone.

About Bytom's DeFi protocol MOV:

Bytom's DeFi protocol MOV is comprised of a trading protocol and an Asset Lending protocol. With layer 2 sidechain technology, MOV can support a vast array of Financial products. MOV is a protocol that is dedicated to constant improvement.
