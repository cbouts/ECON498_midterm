About MATH

MATH price today is $0.481727 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $1,108,197 USD. MATH is up 0.42% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #254, with a market cap of $54,059,026 USD. It has a circulating supply of 112,219,178 MATH coins and a max. supply of 200,000,000 MATH coins. The top exchanges for trading in MATH are currently HBTC, Xtheta Global, Uniswap (V2), BitMart, and Fatbtc. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

MATH describes itself as a one-stop crypto solution platform that comprises MathWallet, MATH VPOS Pool, MathDEX, MATH dApp Store, MATH Staking, MATH Pay, and MATH Chain. Math Wallet is a multi-platform (mobile/desktop/hardware) universal crypto wallet that enables the storage of various cryptoassets, which also supports cross-chain token exchange, and a multi-chain dApp store.
