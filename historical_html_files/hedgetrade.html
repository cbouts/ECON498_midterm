About HedgeTrade

HedgeTrade price today is $1.08 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $609,340 USD. HedgeTrade is up 92.00% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #39, with a market cap of $364,735,586 USD. It has a circulating supply of 339,231,468 HEDG coins and the max. supply is not available. The top exchanges for trading in HedgeTrade are currently CoinTiger, VCC Exchange, HitBTC, Bitcoin.com Exchange, and Uniswap (V2). You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

HedgeTrade aims to become a platform where the world’s best traders share their knowledge. Traders post predictions into a smart contract-powered Blueprint that users can purchase or unlock in order to access. Traders are rewarded if the Blueprint is correct otherwise the user's purchase is refunded. HedgeTrade aims to revolutionize social trading using blockchain technology.
