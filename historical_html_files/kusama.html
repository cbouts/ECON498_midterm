About Kusama

Kusama price today is $29.14 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $27,257,046 USD. Kusama is up 2.19% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #56, with a market cap of $246,797,974 USD. It has a circulating supply of 8,470,098 KSM coins and the max. supply is not available. The top exchanges for trading in Kusama are currently Binance, Huobi Global, Binance.KR, HBTC, and OKEx. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

Kusama is an early, unaudited, and unrefined release of Polkadot. Kusama aims to serve as a proving ground, allowing teams and developers to build and deploy a parachain or try out Polkadot’s governance, staking, nomination, and validation functionality in a real environment.
