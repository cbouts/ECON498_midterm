About Poseidon Network

Poseidon Network price today is $0.094324 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $1,419,439 USD. Poseidon Network is up 3.62% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #217, with a market cap of $203,068,704 USD. It has a circulating supply of 2,152,875,408 QQQ coins and the max. supply is not available. The top exchanges for trading in Poseidon Network are currently Hotbit, and BitForex. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

Poseidon Network provides decentralized edge solutions aimed at creating a worldwide P2P infrastructure. This includes decentralized CDN, storage, and computing. Users can provide their unused bandwidth and storage from their devices, such as NAS, computers, and mobile devices. Poseidon's platform network collects and re-allocates these resources to users who need them. It utilizes idle resources and allows users to share those resources in exchange for rewards.
