About QuickX Protocol

QuickX Protocol price today is $0.018464 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $422,646 USD. QuickX Protocol is up 2.87% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #505, with a market cap of $9,201,010 USD. It has a circulating supply of 498,334,428 QCX coins and the max. supply is not available. The top exchanges for trading in QuickX Protocol are currently Hotbit, BitMax, and Bilaxy. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

QuickX describes itself as a protocol designed specifically to address the issues that are hindering the mass adoption of cryptocurrencies. It reportedly performs transactions off the chain and utilizes pooling facilitators who supply liquidity for cross-chain transfers of crypto assets. Through this, it aims to accelerate transaction speeds and promote interoperability between different cryptocurrencies.
