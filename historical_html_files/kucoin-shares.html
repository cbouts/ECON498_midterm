About KuCoin Shares

KuCoin Shares price today is $0.834181 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $5,128,703 USD. KuCoin Shares is up 0.28% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #107, with a market cap of $67,342,114 USD. It has a circulating supply of 80,728,394 KCS coins and the max. supply is not available. The top exchanges for trading in KuCoin Shares are currently BitMax, KuCoin, and ProBit Exchange. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

Kucoin Shares (KCS) is an exchange based token and native currency of the Kucoin crypto exchange. Holders of the token are able to reap the rewards from the success of the exchange as 50% of its overall trading fee revenue is shared with holders through the tokens. Rewards, received in the form of dividends, are proportionate to the amount of Kucoin Shares one holds.
