About Qcash

Qcash price today is $0.152131 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $443,425,768 USD. Qcash is up 0.94% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #239, with a market cap of $69,980,367 USD. It has a circulating supply of 460,000,200 QC coins and a max. supply of 10,000,000,000 QC coins.

Qcash is a stablecoin that aims to mirror the value of the CNY(Chinese Yuan). The idea was to create a stable cryptocurrency that can be used like digital CNY. The price of 1 Qcash is 1 CNY. Users can use 1 CNY to buy 1 QC and then use QC to trade cryptoassets.
