About BIKI

BIKI price today is $0.029151 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $1,055,676 USD. BIKI is up 0.55% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #504, with a market cap of $9,179,744 USD. It has a circulating supply of 314,898,567 BIKI coins and a max. supply of 1,000,000,000 BIKI coins. The top exchanges for trading in BIKI are currently BiONE, BiKi, Coinsbit, and Uniswap (V2). You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

BIKI is a token issued by the BiKi trading platform, and represents the rights and interests of the exchange. BIKI proposed the 'extreme deflation' model, which entails the 'daily repurchase, quarterly destruction, and lockup" of its asset.
