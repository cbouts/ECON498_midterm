About BTSE

BTSE price today is $2.26 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $157,156 USD. BTSE is down 1.74% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #485, with a market cap of $9,827,689 USD. It has a circulating supply of 4,354,711 BTSE coins and a max. supply of 200,000,000 BTSE coins. The top exchanges for trading in BTSE are currently Bitfinex, BTSE, Uniswap (V2), and ExMarkets. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

Launched on 05/05/2020, BTSE aims provides an exchange utility token carefully designed to augment the user experience on the exchange and within the BTSE ecosystem. BTSE is the first exchange token running on Liquid, a Bitcoin sidechain-based settlement network for traders and exchanges and includes Confidential Transactions and Confidential Assets to maximize on-chain privacy.
