Revain is blockchain-based review platform for crypto community. Since 2018 the platform accumulated the biggest number of user feedback on crypto on the Internet.

Revain consists of 7 main sections: projects, exchanges, wallets, games, casinos, mining pools and cards. In each section we rank every company based on their user rating and number of reviews.

Revain's ultimate goal is to provide high-quality and authentic user feedback on all global products and services using new emerging technologies like blockchain and machine learning.
