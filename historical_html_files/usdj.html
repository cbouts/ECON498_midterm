About USDJ

USDJ price today is $0.991954 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $4,897,363 USD. USDJ is up 0.15% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #451, with a market cap of $11,449,113 USD. It has a circulating supply of 11,541,985 USDJ coins and the max. supply is not available. The top exchanges for trading in USDJ are currently BitZ, Hoo, BiKi, MXC.COM, and KuCoin. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

In the whitepaper, USDJ is described as a a USD-pegged cryptocurrency backed by collateral assets, generated through decentralized smart contracts on the TRON network. "Anyone can pledge TRX as collateral to generate USDJ." It is reportedly pegged to the US dollar through Collateralized Debt Positions (CDPs), and has autonomous feedback mechanisms.
