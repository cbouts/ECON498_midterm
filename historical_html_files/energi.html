About Energi

Energi price today is $1.51 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $2,142,836 USD. Energi is up 2.00% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #129, with a market cap of $51,992,503 USD. It has a circulating supply of 34,528,349 NRG coins and the max. supply is not available. The top exchanges for trading in Energi are currently HitBTC, DigiFinex, Uniswap (V2), WhiteBIT, and Hotbit. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

Energi (NRG) is a next-generation Proof of Stake (PoS) cryptocurrency that combines smart contract capabilities, decentralized governance, and a self-funding treasury. Energi was founded by Tommy World Power, who imagined a better, sustainable cryptocurrency that had the potential for mass adoption. The project has since grown to more than 60 team members and 225,000 community members, all working together to create a cryptocurrency for the future. Learn more at https://www.energi.world/
