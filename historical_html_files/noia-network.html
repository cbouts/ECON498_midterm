About NOIA Network

NOIA Network price today is $0.045684 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $466,822 USD. NOIA Network is down 1.76% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #388, with a market cap of $14,682,595 USD. It has a circulating supply of 321,395,830 NOIA coins and a max. supply of 1,000,000,000 NOIA coins. The top exchanges for trading in NOIA Network are currently Uniswap (V2), Bilaxy, KuCoin, and 0x Protocol. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

NOIA Network aims to utilize Segment Routing, IPv6, and Distributed Ledger technology to create a global Software Defined Network (SDN) on the public Internet.

NOIA’s 'programmable internet' reportedly allows ISPs, data centers, and individual infrastructure providers to connect to Distributed Ledgers and sell their infrastructure as internet transit. The NOIA Platform aims to be the world’s first 'Programmable Internet Backbone As a Service'.
