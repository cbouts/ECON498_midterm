About Request

Request price today is $0.019409 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $148,197 USD. Request is up 9.48% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #332, with a market cap of $19,408,525 USD. It has a circulating supply of 999,966,001 REQ coins and a max. supply of 999,983,984 REQ coins. The top exchanges for trading in Request are currently Binance, Huobi Global, OKEx, Bitfinex, and Uniswap (V2). You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

The Y-Combinator backed Request Network (REQ) bills itself as a decentralized network for payment requests.

The request can be detected and paid in a secure way, without the need for a third party. Requests are saved on an immutable ledger, which acts as a source of truth for accounting and auditing.
