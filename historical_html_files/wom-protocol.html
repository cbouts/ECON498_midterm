About WOM Protocol

WOM Protocol price today is $0.208922 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $2,603,124 USD. WOM Protocol is up 7.45% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #316, with a market cap of $20,935,531 USD. It has a circulating supply of 100,207,450 WOM coins and a max. supply of 1,000,000,000 WOM coins. The top exchanges for trading in WOM Protocol are currently Liquid, Bithumb, KuCoin, and Bithumb Global. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

Launched at the beginning of 2018 by a team based in Berlin and Malta (now Singapore), the WOM Protocol is a MarTech solution (WOM Protocol & WOM Token) that enables brands to access genuine word-of-mouth recommendations. The WOM Protocol provides a way to reward creators for their product-referring content, with the goal of not compromising consumer trust in the content and its creators. The first platform utilizing the WOM Protocol and rewarding users for recommendations is the YEAY app.
