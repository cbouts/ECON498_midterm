About TNC Coin

TNC Coin price today is $0.104978 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $11,383,412 USD. TNC Coin is up 1.22% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #214, with a market cap of $242,543,210 USD. It has a circulating supply of 2,310,424,689 TNC coins and a max. supply of 1,000,000,000,000 TNC coins. The top exchanges for trading in TNC Coin are currently CoinBene, CoinTiger, VCC Exchange, HitBTC, and DigiFinex. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

TNC Coin is the official digital currency of TNC IT Solutions Group. It serves as an integral part of all the transactions and processes within the TNC ecosystem.

The TNC Coin is developed for two main purposes: (a) it will be used for the token swap process as part of the Crypto M&A, and (b) it will be part of an open-source network where gaming developers can fork and create TNC-20 standard gaming tokens by building gaming programs on the TNC mainnet.
