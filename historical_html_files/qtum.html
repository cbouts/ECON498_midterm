About Qtum

Qtum price today is $1.83 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $674,048,818 USD. Qtum is down 1.30% in the last 24 hours. The top exchanges for trading in Qtum are currently XT, Binance, Huobi Global, IDCM, and Binance.KR. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

Qtum (QTUM) is a Proof-of-Stake (PoS) smart contract blockchain platform and value transfer protocol. In PoS, node operators are rewarded for validating transactions. Qtum is built on Bitcoin's UTXO transaction model, with the added functionality of smart contract execution.
