About Theta Fuel

Theta Fuel price today is $0.008513 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $1,619,504 USD. Theta Fuel is down 0.35% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #142, with a market cap of $42,246,435 USD. It has a circulating supply of 4,962,631,792 TFUEL coins and the max. supply is not available. The top exchanges for trading in Theta Fuel are currently Binance, Binance.KR, Upbit, Hotbit, and Gate.io. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

Theta Fuel is the second token (gas token) of the Theta Network. Theta Fuel is a native token on the Theta blockchain. TFUEL powers on-chain operations like payments to relayers for sharing a video stream, or for deploying or interacting with smart contracts. Relayers earn TFUEL for every video stream they relay to other users on the network.
