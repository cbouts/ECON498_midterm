About XeniosCoin

XeniosCoin price today is $0.472459 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $74,689.74 USD. XeniosCoin is up 4.13% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #270, with a market cap of $36,036,795 USD. It has a circulating supply of 76,274,958 XNC coins and a max. supply of 110,000,000 XNC coins. The top exchanges for trading in XeniosCoin are currently VinDAX, Folgory, Finexbox, and Altilly. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

Launched on 20/10/2019 by a team based in Greece. XeniosCoin is a POW/POS digital currency. XeniosCoin describes itself as a business and asset management-oriented cryptocurrency. It aims to offer anonymous, secure, and fast transactions.

Xenios Coin (XNC) is designed to be a digital currency with real use. Starting with the support of the Hellenic Blockchain Association, it will be used as the official currency for yearly member subscriptions to the association.
