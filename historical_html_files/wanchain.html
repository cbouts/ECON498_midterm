About Wanchain

Wanchain price today is $0.223111 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $682,896 USD. Wanchain is up 3.21% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #151, with a market cap of $37,657,135 USD. It has a circulating supply of 168,781,815 WAN coins and the max. supply is not available. The top exchanges for trading in Wanchain are currently Binance, Huobi Global, DragonEX, BKEX, and Bitrue. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

Wanchain aims to be the infrastructure connecting the decentralized financial world. Wanchain’s live cross-blockchain solution is EVM-based, includes optional private transactions, and provides a decentralized, permissionless, and secure approach for interoperability. Having integrated Bitcoin, Ethereum, and EOS, Wanchain aims to pave the way for plug and play connectivity between public, private, and consortia blockchains. Wanchain has employees globally with teams in Beijing (China), Austin (USA), Kuala Lumpur (Malaysia), and Madrid (Spain).
