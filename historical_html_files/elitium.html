About Elitium

Elitium price today is $1.14 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $113,431 USD. Elitium is up 2.53% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #336, with a market cap of $19,314,704 USD. It has a circulating supply of 16,915,528 EUM coins and the max. supply is not available. The top exchanges for trading in Elitium are currently BitMart, Fatbtc, and STEX. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

Elitium describes itself as an app-based marketplace that puts a world of luxury goods and services in the palm of the user's hand. It aims to use smart contracts, AI, and cryptocurrency to remove the inefficiencies of the luxury industry. The native cryptocurrency of Elitium is EUM -- a utility coin that reportedly enables secure, fast, and high-volume payments within the Elitium marketplace.
