About Mixin

Mixin price today is $131.52 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $1,047,244 USD. Mixin is down 0.24% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #238, with a market cap of $68,814,424 USD. It has a circulating supply of 523,208 XIN coins and a max. supply of 1,000,000 XIN coins. The top exchanges for trading in Mixin are currently BitZ, Uniswap (V2), BigONE, and CBX. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

Mixin (XIN) bills itself as a free and lightning fast peer-to-peer transactional network for digital assets. Leveraging Directed Acyclic Graph (DAG) and Byzantine Fault-Tolerant protocols, Mixin aspires to help other blockchains 'achieve trillions of TPS, sub-second final confirmations, zero transaction fees, enhanced privacy, and unlimited extensibility'.
