About PlayFuel

PlayFuel price today is $0.056713 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $607,966 USD. PlayFuel is down 3.13% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #286, with a market cap of $28,356,561 USD. It has a circulating supply of 500,000,000 PLF coins and a max. supply of 10,000,000,000 PLF coins. The top exchanges for trading in PlayFuel are currently CoinBene, VCC Exchange, DigiFinex, LBank, and BigONE. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

PlayFuel aims to bring the power of blockchain technology to the gaming world. It is creating a platform that allows developers to earn funding by integrating PlayFuel into their games and allowing players to mine PLF just by playing. Players can use these tokens to buy games, redeem exclusive goodies, or even exchange the tokens for real money.

Developers can gain funding through gamers who purchase PLF and invest in the development of their games. Investors get access to beta testing, exclusive in-game items, and will be one of the first users to be given access to the actual game.
