About Cortex

Cortex price today is $0.074386 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $1,793,571 USD. Cortex is down 1.58% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #338, with a market cap of $18,591,874 USD. It has a circulating supply of 249,938,725 CTXC coins and a max. supply of 299,792,458 CTXC coins. The top exchanges for trading in Cortex are currently Binance, Huobi Global, OKEx, CoinBene, and DragonEX. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

Cortex is an open-source, peer-to-peer, decentralized blockchain platform that supports Artificial Intelligence (AI) models to be uploaded and executed on the distributed network. Cortex provides an open-source AI platform to achieve AI democratization where models can be integrated easily in smart contracts and create AI-enable decentralized applications (DApps).
