About Paxos Standard

Paxos Standard price today is $0.998481 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $235,922,946 USD. Paxos Standard is down 0.03% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #57, with a market cap of $244,579,795 USD. It has a circulating supply of 244,951,954 PAX coins and the max. supply is not available. The top exchanges for trading in Paxos Standard are currently XT, Binance, Huobi Global, CITEX, and OKEx. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

Paxos Standard (PAX) is a stablecoin that allows users to exchange US dollars for Paxos Standard Tokens to 'transact at the speed of the internet'. It aims to meld the stability of the dollar with blockchain technology. Paxos, the company behind PAX, has a charter from the New York State Department of Financial Services, which allows it to offer regulated services in the cryptoasset space.
