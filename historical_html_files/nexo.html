About Nexo

Nexo price today is $0.177746 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $9,567,542 USD. Nexo is down 0.09% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #84, with a market cap of $99,537,881 USD. It has a circulating supply of 560,000,011 NEXO coins and the max. supply is not available. The top exchanges for trading in Nexo are currently Huobi Global, HitBTC, Hoo, BiONE, and UPEX. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

NEXO (NEXO) is a compliant token that is backed by the underlying assets of Nexo’s loan portfolio. NEXO reportedly provides regular passive income to token holders in the form of 30% from the company’s profits. The latest dividend payout to NEXO Token Holders on August 15, 2019 was reportedly for $2,409,574.87. The token also offers discounted interest rates (-50%) on Nexo’s Instant Loans. The NEXO Token can also be used as collateral on the platform (https://nexo.io) in addition to BTC, ETH, XRP, LTC, NEXO, BNB and several major stablecoins.

Most recently, Nexo has launched the much-anticipated Earn on Crypto, giving clients the opportunity to earn up to 5% interest on their crypto, including BTC, ETH, XRP, XLM, LTC, BCH, EOS, LINK and up to 10% on their stablecoins and fiat, including USDT, USDC, DAI, TUSD, PAX, EUR and GBP.
