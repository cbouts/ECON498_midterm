About Vitae

Vitae price today is $6.99 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $1,270,387 USD. Vitae is up 7.27% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #222, with a market cap of $136,182,682 USD. It has a circulating supply of 19,471,178 VITAE coins and a max. supply of 100,000,000 VITAE coins. The top exchanges for trading in Vitae are currently HitBTC, and STEX. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

Launched in 2018 by a team based in Switzerland, Vitae Token is a PivX fork. It utilizes a network of Masternodes/ Supernodes for decentralized governance and increased transaction privacy. Its motto is growing global prosperity.
