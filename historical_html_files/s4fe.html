About S4FE

S4FE price today is $0.040605 USD with a 24-hour trading volume of $551,417 USD. S4FE is up 1.32% in the last 24 hours. The current CoinMarketCap ranking is #267, with a market cap of $36,354,286 USD. It has a circulating supply of 895,310,762 S4F coins and the max. supply is not available. The top exchanges for trading in S4FE are currently Uniswap (V2), LATOKEN, Exrates, and Mercatox. You can find others listed on our crypto exchanges page.

S4FE claims to be the world’s first blockchain-based global database for the registration and identification of stolen and lost items. The database is accessible to everyone and enables users to register and search for property. The types of property include the likes of electronic devices, jewelry, personal collections, documents, vehicles, and any item representing value to its owner.
